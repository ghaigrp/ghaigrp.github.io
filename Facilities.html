Our Facilities

Ghai Group is a dynamic organization with a passion for high quality service and excellence. With an in-house manufacturing facility and a modern corporate office, we are committed to being a leading trader of Industrial Hardware & cutting tools, Abrasives, Adhesives, and Medical Devices of prestigious brands in India and overseas.

Manufacturing Unit

For nearly three decades, our family-owned hardware venture, established in 1994, has been dedicated to assisting individuals in accomplishing their projects with top-notch products and outstanding service. Founded by Tarun Ghai and Jaideep Ghai, sons of Onkar Ghai, our commitment remains unwavering. Our journey began with a vision to be a reliable one-stop destination for all hardware needs, and today, we take pride in our strong relationships with customers, built on trust and satisfaction. As we continue to grow, we remain grateful for the support and loyalty of our valued patrons.
